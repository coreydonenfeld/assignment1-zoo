Pandas

The Panda is a smart and cute animal.

About Learn about our Pandas

The giant panda, is a bear species endemic to China. It is characterised by its bold black-and-white coat and rotund body. The name "giant panda" is sometimes used to distinguish it from the red panda, a neighboring musteloid.
